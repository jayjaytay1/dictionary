Car Cost Tracker

In Development

Owning a car costs far more than the sticker price and the fuel gauge lets on. Car Cost Tracker brings every recurring and one-off expense into a single, clear picture — so everyday drivers can finally answer the question "what is this vehicle actually costing me?" It's designed to be quick to log, honest with the numbers, and genuinely useful when it's time to decide whether to keep, sell, or replace a car.

Planned features
  • Expense trackingLog fuel, maintenance, insurance, tax, and one-off costs in seconds.
  • Maintenance remindersStay ahead of services, MOTs, and renewals before they're overdue.
  • Cost breakdownsSee where your money goes — by category, by month, and cost-per-mile.
  • ReportsClear summaries you can review, export, and actually act on.
